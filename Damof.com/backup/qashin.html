DAMOF Community Development

(Development and Advancement for Marginalized and Oppressed Communities) is a dedicated registered non-profit organization working tirelessly to bring positive change and development to the SSC-Khatumo region, situated in the northern part of Somalia.

Objectives

  1. Enhance Education: To improve access to quality education, raise literacy rates, and provide vocational training opportunities for youth in the SSC-Khatumo Region.
  2. Promote Healthcare: To ensure access to essential healthcare services, increase awareness of health issues, and strengthen local healthcare infrastructure.
  3. Economic Empowerment: To create sustainable economic opportunities through skills development, entrepreneurship support, and microfinance initiatives.
  4. Social Inclusion: To foster social cohesion and inclusion by addressing gender disparities, advocating for the rights of marginalized groups, and promoting community-based initiatives.

Mission

Mission Statement: At Damof NGO, our mission is to spearhead a transformative journey towards sustainable community development within the SSC-Khatumo Region, situated in the northern reaches of Somalia. We ardently embrace the responsibility of confronting the unique and pressing needs faced by marginalized and oppressed communities. Our resolute dedication revolves around the profound empowerment of these communities through multifaceted initiatives encompassing education, healthcare, economic enfranchisement, and the promotion of social inclusivity.

Vision:

We envision a future where every individual in the SSC-Khatumo Region enjoys access to essential services, equal opportunities, and a dignified life, irrespective of their background or circumstance. Through holistic development initiatives, we aim to create resilient, self-sufficient communities that can thrive independently.

Where we work

Our primary geographic focus is the SSC-Khatumo Region in North Somalia, an area with a history of marginalization and conflict. By concentrating our efforts in this region, we aim to bring about meaningful and sustainable change to improve the lives of its residents. Impact: To date, Damof NGO has positively impacted thousands of lives in the SSC-Khatumo Region through various initiatives, including improved access to education, healthcare, economic opportunities, and social inclusion programs. Through our tireless dedication to fostering sustainable community development, we are determined to continue making a difference and building a brighter future for the people of the SSC-Khatumo Region, North of Somalia.

About Damof NGO

Damof NGO is a dedicated community development organization operating in the SSC-Khatumo Region, situated in the northern part of Somalia. With a profound commitment to fostering positive change and sustainable development, Damof NGO has been actively working in this region to address the unique challenges faced by marginalized communities.

Core Values

  • Equity: We believe in the inherent worth and potential of every individual, and we work tirelessly to ensure equal access to opportunities and resources for all.
  • Empowerment: We empower communities to take charge of their own development by providing the knowledge, tools, and support they need to drive positive change.
  • Partnership: We forge strong collaborations with local, national, and international stakeholders to maximize our impact and leverage resources effectively.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to sustainable practices, both in our projects and in our organization, to ensure a lasting impact on the communities we serve.
  • Inclusivity: We champion diversity and inclusivity, respecting the unique cultures and traditions of the communities we work with.
