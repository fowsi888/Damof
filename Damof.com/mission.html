Mission

Mission Statement: At Damof NGO, our mission is to spearhead a transformative journey towards sustainable community development within the SSC-Khatumo Region, situated in the northern reaches of Somalia. We ardently embrace the responsibility of confronting the unique and pressing needs faced by marginalized and oppressed communities. Our resolute dedication revolves around the profound empowerment of these communities through multifaceted initiatives encompassing education, healthcare, economic enfranchisement, and the promotion of social inclusivity.

Vision:

We envision a future where every individual in the SSC-Khatumo Region enjoys access to essential services, equal opportunities, and a dignified life, irrespective of their background or circumstance. Through holistic development initiatives, we aim to create resilient, self-sufficient communities that can thrive independently.
